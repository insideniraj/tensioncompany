Our Journey With Startups

1

Seed Stage

We identify promising startups at their earliest stages, often when they're just an idea or prototype. Our initial investments range from $500K to $5M for 15-30% equity.

2

Growth & Scaling

We provide follow-on funding (typically $5M-$50M) and strategic guidance to help startups refine their product, build their team, and establish market fit.

3

Maturity & Exit

Once the company reaches stability (typically 5-10 years), we exit through IPO or acquisition, typically realizing 20-100x returns on our initial investment.

Our Funding Process

1

Initial Application

Submit your pitch through our online portal. We review all applications within 2 weeks. Focus on clearly articulating your vision, technology, and market opportunity.

2

Technical Deep Dive

Selected startups undergo a 4-6 week technical evaluation with our team of domain experts. We'll assess your technology, team, and market potential in detail.

3

Investment Committee

Our investment team presents your startup to our partners. This includes a comprehensive review of all findings and a recommendation for funding.

4

Term Sheet & Funding

Approved startups receive a term sheet within 1 week of committee approval. Funding typically follows 2-4 weeks after signed agreements.
